Lead Python Developer New
TalentStack Ltd. Budapest, Budapest, Hungary

Job Title

Lead Python Developer

Job Description

You will:

  • Lead the design and implementation of large-scale, complex software systems
  • Act as the owner of the system
  • Need in-depth knowledge of design patterns, software architecture and design principles and how to implement them.
  • Need to be familiar with common technologies used in distributed systems as well the use and maintenance of database systems.
  • Need strong knowledge of Python 3 and associated libraries.
  • Write unit tests and document all code.
  • Need a solid understanding of the development process pipeline, practicing effective code version control, as well as Continuous Integration and Deployment.

Restrictions

  • No telecommuting
  • No Agencies Please

Requirements

About the ideal candidate: You will fit in if you think in code, aspire to tackle undiscovered computer science challenges and are motivated by being around like-minded people. Fun fact: of the 600 employees globally, approximately 500 of them code on a daily basis.

Tech skills:

  • Degree in a quantitative or technical discipline (strong mathematical background)
  • 10+ years of experience as a developer with experience in developing distributed systems
  • Experience with mentoring and guiding more junior team members
  • Black-belt Python programmer (experience with Numpy, Pandas, Django and Asyncio)
  • Working knowledge in various database (MySQL, MongoDB) and messaging technologies (SQL, Redis, Kafka, Cassandra)
  • Proven track record of leading fast paced, complex projects, and providing technical leadership to other software developers within and across the organization

About the Company

About the company:

Our partner is developing systemical financial strategies, producing high-quality predictive signals (alphas) through their proprietary research platform.

What to expect:

  • Relaxed yet intellectually intense environment
  • Lean and agile teams, immediate user feedback
  • You’re free to bring your individual, unconventional ideas
  • Thorough selection process
  • Working together with top-notch developers
